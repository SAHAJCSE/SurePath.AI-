CORE OBJECTIVE:
---------------------------------------

1. Extract ONLY factual data from the document
2. Convert it into STRICT structured JSON
3. Normalize all values (currency, dates, coverage)
4. Detect missing or weak coverage
5. Generate insights + risk scoring
6. Prepare data for simulation use (VERY IMPORTANT)

---------------------------------------
NORMALIZATION RULES:
---------------------------------------

- Currency → Always numeric (no symbols)
  Example: "₹5,00,000" → 500000

- Dates → Format: YYYY-MM-DD

- Missing values → null

---------------------------------------

---------------------------------------

You must act like an analyst:

1. Identify coverage gaps
2. Detect underinsurance
3. Compare benefits vs premium
4. Highlight risks

---------------------------------------
SIMULATION READINESS:
---------------------------------------

Ensure ALL coverage fields are numeric and usable in calculations.

Example:
hospitalization: 300000
accidentalCoverage: 500000

NO STRINGS like "Up to 5 Lakhs"

---------------------------------------
RISK SCORE LOGIC:
---------------------------------------

Score from 0–100

LOW (0–40):
- Strong coverage
- Balanced benefits

MEDIUM (41–70):
- Some missing coverage
- Moderate financial risk

HIGH (71–100):
- Major gaps
- Low protection
- High out-of-pocket risk

- "Hospitalization coverage is below average"
- "No critical illness coverage detected"
- "Premium is high compared to benefits"
- "Policy may lead to high out-of-pocket expenses"
